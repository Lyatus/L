# Sample

## Controls

- WASD/ZQSD to move the camera
- Left shift to slow movement
- Mouse to rotate
- Left click to throw cubes
- B to play/stop Bach (ogg)
- G to play/stop a guitar sound (wav)
- M to play/stop Mozart (midi)
